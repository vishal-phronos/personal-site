PLOS Computational Biology · 2013

Network Signatures of Survival

My first major publication. CRANE (Combinatorially dysRegulated subNEtworks) found patterns in protein-protein interaction networks that predict patient outcomes.

A 50-gene "subnetwork signature" achieved 80% accuracy in predicting glioblastoma survival—and translated to protein-level changes 6.5× better than traditional approaches.
